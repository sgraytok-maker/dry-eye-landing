Common Questions About Dry Eye Treatment:

What causes chronic dry eye syndrome?
Dry eye syndrome can result from aging, hormonal changes, medications, environmental factors, or blocked meibomian glands. Our comprehensive evaluation identifies your specific cause for targeted treatment.
How effective is OptiLight IPL for dry eye relief?
OptiLight IPL therapy has shown excellent results, with most patients experiencing significant dry eye relief after 3-4 treatments. It's FDA-approved and addresses the root cause of dry eye disease.
How long does dry eye treatment take?
Treatment duration varies by individual, but most patients begin experiencing dry eye relief within 2-3 weeks of starting treatment. Complete improvement typically occurs over 2-3 months.
